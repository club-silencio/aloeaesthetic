Welcome to Our New Home!

We are proud and excited to share our beautiful new space at 300 Vesey, and hope that you will love it. We’ve flexed our Experiential expertise to make this the best experience and transition for our Momentum family. It’s not just a new, gorgeous, open-concept office with a rooftop terrace and sweeping river views… It’s a new stage for us to collaborate, better serve our clients and grow.

We are creative. We are tactile. We've been called "the smartest people in the room with dirt under our fingernails." We love that! This is the Momentum way, and this is the way we work. We are now in a space designed to highlight and support our strengths and who we are as an agency. 

300 Vesey marks the next incredible chapter for Momentum Worldwide, and is also the first time that we have been able to build our space from scratch to truly reflect our unique agency persona. This is an environment built to foster creativity and performance, one that allows for flexibility, choice, transparency and connectivity. Beyond the increased number of bookable conference and phone rooms, there are now 50 breakout areas and collaboration spots for any meeting needs throughout the day.

Our Office: Conference Room Etiquette

  • Plan ahead of time and reach out to IT and/or Office Services respectively for moving furniture, technology, special setups, etc.
  • Clean up any materials and trash.
  • Remove any notes or business material.
  • Call or email IT and/or Office Services if something is broken, or does not work properly.
  • Please monitor your time, and end meetings accordingly.
  • Return cables, adapters/dongles, phone and/or keyboard to appropriate spots.
