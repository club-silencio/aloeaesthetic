introduction

We are pleased to invite you and a guest to join us for the 82nd Annual Partner Appreciation Program at Pebble Beach Resorts – a Chevron legacy event.

At Chevron, our success is driven by our people and their commitment to getting results the right way. As our valued partner, you are an integral part of our continued commitment to human progress and harnessing the power of human energy®.

In recognition of our joint successes, please join us for an unforgettable weekend to celebrate our partnership as we continue to power the world forward.

Location:
Casa Palmero® at Pebble Beach Resorts
1518 Cypress Drive
Pebble Beach, CA 93959

Date:
February 16-19, 2018

hotel

Chevron has reserved one room for you and your guest at Casa Palmero, located on the Pebble Beach Resorts property. Guests will be responsible for providing a credit card upon arrival to cover incidentals.

activities

Golf tee times have already been booked and no action is required once you accept the invitation. Companions who would like to book spa appointments will be provided a contact name and number for Chevron's dedicated spa concierge at Casa Palmero. This contact information will be provided once you have accepted the invitation to attend the event.

travel

All guests will be responsible for making their own travel arrangements. Please include your travel details on your registration form so we may arrange airport transfers. If your travel arrangements change, please send your updated travel details to humanenergy@chevronevents.com.

attire

The attire is business casual. This is defined as a more dressed-up look than classic casual, but not nearly as formal as traditional office clothes.

registration

If you can attend, please go to the registration tab to submit your information. Please note that due to the nature of this business event, all guests must be 21 years or older. Registration must be completed by January 19, 2018.

itinerary

Please visit the itinerary tab to see a schedule of events throughout the weekend.

If you have any questions, please contact humanenergy@chevronevents.com.
